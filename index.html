ABOUT

Hi, I'm Siyamthanda Mazokwana, a UI/UX Designer from Cape Town with a growing interest in digital design. My passion lies in creating visually appealing and user-friendly digital experiences. I recently graduated from UCT GSB Solution Space's developer programme, enhancing my skills in web development and design. Currently, I'm applying these skills to an exciting Township Property Listing App project. My strength is bridging design and functionality, always keeping the user in mind. I enjoy tackling complex problems and turning them into simple, beautiful solutions. I'm always eager to learn and take on new challenges in the ever-evolving world of digital design and marketing.
